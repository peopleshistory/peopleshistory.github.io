About Us

A People’s History is a documentary podcast about class struggle in the United States. Inspired by sections from Howard Zinn's landmark book, each six-episode season covers one local story, told from the viewpoint of common people.

Alejandro Ramirez is a freelance writer based in Boston and the editor of Spare Change News. He grew up in Lawrence, Massachusetts. Conor Gillies grew up in Maine and studied history at Boston University. He produces for Radio Open Source. Qainat Khan grew up in Fresno, California. She has produced news for Morning Edition and is an editor for GroundTruth at WGBH. Alison Bruzek is a radio producer and science writer. She has worked on Radio Boston and is now a reporter at KUOW in Seattle.

Editing help from Ben Shapiro, David Wallis, and Alissa Quart. Theme music by Marisa Anderson, original score by Liz Harris and Ilyas Ahmed. Major production help from Rosie Busiakiewicz, Rehanna Fernandes, Ed Paget, Patrick King, Cait Gillies, and Alex Silva.
